## Simple, Smart, and Compliant Accounting

Sumu takes the complexity out of accounting. Built for small and medium businesses, our bilingual (Arabic–English) accounting tools help you stay compliant and organized—without needing to be a finance expert.

## Key Features

- **General Ledger** – Keep all transactions organized and accessible
- **VAT Automation** – Real-time tax calculation and compliance with GCC regulations
- **Expense Tracking** – Categorize expenses, upload receipts, and monitor spending
- **Cash Flow Management** – Track income and expenses at a glance
- **Bank Reconciliation** – Sync and reconcile accounts automatically
- **Multi-Currency Support** – Manage transactions in local and international currencies
- **Financial Reports** – Generate P&L statements, balance sheets, and tax summaries

## Why Choose Sumu?

- **Bilingual & Localized** – Arabic–English interfaces for the GCC business environment
- **Compliance-First** – Always aligned with VAT and financial reporting requirements
- **Time-Saving** – Automate repetitive bookkeeping tasks
- **Insight-Driven** – Real-time reports for confident decision-making

## Complete Accounting Solution

Double-entry bookkeeping, automatic balancing, profit and loss reports, VAT-compatible invoicing, cash flow tracking, balance sheet generation, and seamless data import from other software.
