## Double Entry Accounting System

Sumu accounting enables double-entry bookkeeping, where each transaction is recorded in both debit and credit accounts to maintain balance and ensure accuracy.

- **Accurate Journal Entries**: Every transaction is automatically logged in the corresponding accounts, ensuring that journal entries remain balanced and follow standard accounting principles.
- **Built-in Ledger Management**: Sumu's ledger feature maintains individual daily entries for all parties, assets, and liabilities. This helps business owners track account balances efficiently.
- **Automatic Balancing**: The platform ensures all debits and credits match automatically, significantly reducing the chance of misstatements in financial statements or books.

## Profit and Loss Reports

Sumu allows you to generate real-time Profit and Loss (P&L) statements that display the income and expense overview for your business across selected timeframes.

- **Real-Time Tracking**: Users can track income and expenses daily, weekly, or monthly, making it easy to evaluate business performance over time.
- **Expense Categorization**: Sumu expense management lets you tag each expense under predefined categories like rent, utilities, or payroll, enhancing report clarity.
- **Instant Report Generation**: With just a few clicks, generate downloadable P&L reports for sharing with partners, investors, or accountants.

## VAT-Compatible Invoicing and Tax Reports

Sumu small business accounting software enables businesses to create VAT-compliant invoices and generate various reports to simplify tax filing and compliance with the Omani tax laws.

- **Auto VAT Calculations**: Sumu calculates VAT automatically based on party location and item codes, minimizing manual errors.
- **VAT Report Generation**: Automatically prepares VAT reports, making it easier to submit returns via the Tax Authority portal.

## Cash Flow and Payment Tracking

Sumu accounts payable helps you manage outgoing cash, and the receivable feature manages outgoing payments with ease, improving cash flow visibility and payment control.

- **Receivables & Payables Dashboard**: Instantly view outstanding amounts owed by customers and amounts due to vendors from one consolidated screen.
- **Payment Modes Tracking**: Whether it is mobile payment, cash, or bank transfers, Sumu records every payment mode separately, helping with cash flow analysis.
- **Due Date Alerts**: Get automatic alerts for pending payments or receipts, ensuring that you never miss out on follow-ups.
