## Core Accounting Tools

Sumu provides the fundamental accounting components required to maintain structured and compliant financial records.

- **Chart of Accounts**: Create and manage your company's chart of accounts to organize assets, liabilities, equity, revenue, and expenses according to standard accounting practices.
- **Journal Entries**: Record financial transactions through structured journal entries to maintain accurate accounting records and ensure every financial activity is properly documented.
- **VAT and Tax Records**: Maintain proper records of VAT-related transactions and tax obligations in accordance with Oman Tax Authority requirements.
- **Financial Record Management**: Maintain consistent financial records across invoices, payments, and expenses, ensuring that your accounting data remains complete and reliable.
